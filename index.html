Crystals Collector

Instructions

In the "Match this Number" box, you will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals that each have a random value. During a game, each crystal's value won't change. By clicking on a crystal you will add that crystal's value to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you. You'll have to figure out each crystal's value by clicking on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number; you lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

Once you complete a game, a new game will begin. A new random number will be generated and a new random value will be assigned to each crystal.
